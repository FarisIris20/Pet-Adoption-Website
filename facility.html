Our Facilities

The Pet Adoption Centre in Klang Selangor temporarily homes rescues while working to find their new homes under the Managed Adoption Program (MAP). These animals are made up of surrendered pets, rescued cruelty case animals, and stray or abandoned animals brought in by members of the public.




Paw Park

Dog kennels and cat cage, including special maternity and rehabilitation pens

Low-cost spaying/neutering, vaccination, and general care for abandoned and rescued animals

A rainwater-recycling and natural groundwater use facilities

An Adventure park for pets

Pet-friendly areas for privately-booked events
